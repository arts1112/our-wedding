FAQs

What is the dress code?

Guests: We kindly request that guests attend in semi-formal attire that complements our chosen color motif.
Ladies: Please wear modest, flowy dresses that reflect a garden-inspired theme.
Gentlemen: We kindly ask that denim pants be avoided. Black or khaki pants are preferred.

Can I bring someone with me?

Due to limited space and seating availability, we kindly ask for your understanding that only those specifically named on the invitation will be accommodated.

Are children invited?

While we love your little ones, our wedding will be an adults-only celebration.

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive at least 30 minutes before the ceremony begins to allow ample time for seating, a quick restroom break, and greeting fellow guests.

Is there parking available?

A parking attendant will be available to assist you upon arrival at the parking area.

What happens after the ceremony?

A grazing table will be available for light snacks while the couple is having their photoshoot, retouch, and preparing for the reception.

Who can I contact on the day of the event?

Kindly note that the couple will be very busy on the wedding day and may be difficult to reach.
For any questions or concerns, please contact Janine Taburnal (bride’s sister) or Kate David (groom’s sister).
